# Executive and stakeholder briefing

## When to use

- Need a short, decision-oriented summary from a larger body of PM work.
- Aligning tone with [`05_people/communication/`](../../../.productmap/05_people/communication/) and deck/one-pager templates.

## Steps

1. Check [`00_company/product/onepager.md`](../../../.productmap/00_company/product/onepager.md) and [`09_templates/board-deck-template.md`](../../../.productmap/09_templates/board-deck-template.md) for structure hints.
2. Lead with decision/ask, then evidence, options, and recommendation; include risks and what success looks like by date.
3. Keep appendices for detail; state assumptions explicitly.

## Delegation

Use [subagent-prompt.md](subagent-prompt.md) for a sub-agent that specializes in stakeholder-ready briefings.
