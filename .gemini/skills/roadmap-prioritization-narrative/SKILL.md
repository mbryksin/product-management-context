# Roadmap and prioritization narrative

## When to use

- Communicating what ships when and why, beyond a flat feature list.
- Connecting roadmap to strategy, risks, and dependency reality.

## Steps

1. Consult [`00_company/product/roadmap.md`](../../../.productmap/00_company/product/roadmap.md), [`01_strategy/mvp-roadmap/`](../../../.productmap/01_strategy/mvp-roadmap/), and [`08_frameworks/rice.md`](../../../.productmap/08_frameworks/rice.md).
2. Structure **themes**, **time horizons**, **bets**, and **non-goals**; make tradeoffs explicit.
3. Tie items to measurable outcomes where possible; date the narrative.

## Delegation

Use [subagent-prompt.md](subagent-prompt.md) for a sub-agent that produces stakeholder-ready roadmap prose and tables.
