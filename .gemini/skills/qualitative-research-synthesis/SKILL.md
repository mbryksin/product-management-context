# Qualitative research synthesis

## When to use

- Multiple transcripts or notes need thematic synthesis and prioritized implications.
- Outputs should inform problem framing, personas, or backlog without inventing quant claims.

## Steps

1. Orient via [`.productmap/02_generation/user-research/_overview.md`](../../../.productmap/02_generation/user-research/_overview.md) and [problem-framing.md](../../../.productmap/02_generation/user-research/problem-framing.md) where relevant.
2. Tag quotes to themes; separate **evidence** from **interpretation**; flag contradictions.
3. Produce a dated summary: themes, severity/frequency language (qualitative), recommended next research or bets, and links to where raw material should live under `10_data/`.

## Delegation

Use [subagent-prompt.md](subagent-prompt.md) for a sub-agent focused on long-context qualitative synthesis.
