# JTBD and opportunity framing

## When to use

- Moving from solution ideas to job statements and opportunity sizing narratives.
- Aligning discovery with `08_frameworks/jobs-to-be-done.md` and strategy overviews.

## Steps

1. Read [`08_frameworks/jobs-to-be-done.md`](../../../.productmap/08_frameworks/jobs-to-be-done.md) and [`01_strategy/product-market-fit/opportunity-assessment.md`](../../../.productmap/01_strategy/product-market-fit/opportunity-assessment.md) as applicable.
2. Produce job stories, forces of progress, and competing alternatives; separate **job** from **solution**.
3. Link to segmentation/ICP material under `01_strategy/segmentation/` when the user names a market.

## Delegation

Use [subagent-prompt.md](subagent-prompt.md) for a sub-agent focused on JTBD-aligned opportunity docs.
