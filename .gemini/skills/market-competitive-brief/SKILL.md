# Market and competitive brief

## When to use

- Summarizing market structure, competitors, and differentiation for decisions or docs.
- Connecting research to segmentation and business model notes in the repo.

## Steps

1. Draw from [`01_strategy/business-model/_overview.md`](../../../.productmap/01_strategy/business-model/_overview.md), [`01_strategy/segmentation/_overview.md`](../../../.productmap/01_strategy/segmentation/_overview.md), and [`01_strategy/product-market-fit/`](../../../.productmap/01_strategy/product-market-fit/).
2. Structure: landscape, segments, key players, comparison dimensions, gaps/risks, and recommended bets—clearly label hypotheses vs facts.
3. Cite dated sources in the brief; store raw inputs under `10_data/` when applicable.

Use [subagent-prompt.md](subagent-prompt.md) for a sub-agent that produces executive-ready competitive briefs.
