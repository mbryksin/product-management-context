# Product analytics insights

## When to use

- Translating charts/tables into narrative, hypotheses, and next steps.
- Connecting findings to [`03_analysis/analytics/_overview.md`](../../../.productmap/03_analysis/analytics/_overview.md) and KPI material.

## Steps

1. Clarify question, population, time range, and caveats (seasonality, tracking gaps).
2. Separate **observed** vs **inferred**; propose experiments or follow-up analyses with owners.
3. Link recommendations to roadmap/OKRs when context is provided; date the readout.

## Delegation

Use [subagent-prompt.md](subagent-prompt.md) for a sub-agent that produces structured analytics readouts.
